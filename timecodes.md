# Alameda County TAP / HRMS Time Reporting Codes

| Time Code | Description | Category | Notes |
|------------|-------------|-----------|-------|
| `REG` | Regular Hours | Worked Time | Standard hours worked during your scheduled assignment. |
| `OTP` | Overtime | Worked Time | Approved overtime hours worked beyond your regular schedule. |
| `SLA` | Sick Leave – Medical Appointment (Self) | Paid Leave | For your own medical, dental, vision, therapy, or healthcare appointments. |
| `SLS` | Sick Leave – Self Illness / Disability | Paid Leave | For your own illness, injury, recovery, or disability-related absence. |
| `SFC` | Sick Leave – Family (Combined) | Paid Leave | For qualifying family care under Alameda County sick leave policies. |
| `JUR` | Jury Duty | Paid Leave | For approved jury service while assigned and scheduled to work. |
| `*SC` | Schedule Change Code | Administrative | Enter `0.10` hours when a County holiday falls on the first Monday of a pay period, or when you do not work on the first Monday of a pay period. |
